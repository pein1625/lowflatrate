Countertop EMV Terminals

Countertop EMV terminals are at the heart of payment processing. Having a simple countertop terminal equips you to interact process credit cards, print receipts, and get paid. These terminals can accept all forms of payment including chip, swipe, tap, and even mobile wallets. As part of our standard payment processing service, we provide our merchants with free loaner terminals and free thermal paper rolls for receipt printing. Best of all, our service is backed by our 24/7, in-house multilingual support team to make sure you never run into issues with your hardware or your processing service.
